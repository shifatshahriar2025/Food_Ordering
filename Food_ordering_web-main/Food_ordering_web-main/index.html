A burger is a popular fast food consisting of a patty of ground meat (usually beef) placed inside a sliced bun, typically with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments like ketchup or mustard.

Key Ingredients:

  • Burger Bun (white or whole wheat)
  • Beef Patty (or other types of meat like chicken, turkey, or vegetarian options)
  • Cheese (e.g., cheddar, American, Swiss)
  • Vegetables (e.g., lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles)
  • Condiments (e.g., ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)
